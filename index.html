Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the rental?

All necessary headsets, controllers, accessories, and (when selected) on-site support staff. We can also provide additional gear like VR-ready computers or monitors upon request.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance to secure your desired date and ensure proper staffing and equipment availability.

Do I need a large space for VR?

We can tailor the VR setup to your available space. For room-scale setups, a 6’x6’ area per headset is ideal. We’ll guide you on space requirements after discussing your event.

What if I want a custom VR experience?

We have an in-house development team that can build personalized VR content, from branded mini-games to detailed product demos. Just select “Custom Experience” in your booking form.
